  • Our Vision

DIPLOMA Courses (Polytechnic) at LKCE got established in 2019 under the aegis of Khalsa College Lyallpur Education Charitable Trust and affiliated to Punjab State Board of Technical Education and Industrial Training, Chandigarh. It was established with an aim to impart engineering education to the students to make them competent, motivated engineers.

A DIPLOMA Course would help you achieve practical knowledge of training on the basic level. After attaining a polytechnic diploma, you could straightaway apply for jobs in the same field and often in the government or private sectors. Many students even opt for jobs immediately after their polytechnic course gets over, and pursue their degree course while working. So, basically they earn while they learn!

Eligibility Criteria

The candidate should have qualified 10th pass with Mathematics, Science & English subjects and 12th pass in (Science/vocational stream) or ITI course for Diploma – Lateral Entry.

BENEFITS/SCOPE OF DIPLOMA COURSE

• Students, who opt for diplomas are taught the practical and technical part of a course, while degrees are more theoretical. Students completed with diplomas are now in demand for their technical skills.

• Today technical jobs are more available than “office” jobs. Employers want people with practical knowledge rather than theoretical knowledge.

• The technical part taught in diploma courses makes you ready for the job market. The rise in demand for people with technical skills has made things even easier to find a job.
